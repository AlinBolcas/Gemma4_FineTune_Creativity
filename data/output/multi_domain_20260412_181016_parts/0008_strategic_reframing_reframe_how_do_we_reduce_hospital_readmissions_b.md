**Key Questions:**
- **If readmission wasn't measured by 30 days, what key time horizon defines 'success'?**
  *Shifting focus from acute failure to sustained recovery timelines*
- **What are the non-clinical determinants (housing, food security) that predict stability post-discharge?**
  *Identifying social infrastructure gaps as primary failure points*
- **Whose definition of 'good outcome' is prioritized: payer, provider, or patient?**
  *Revealing inherent conflicts of interest in current discharge planning*
- **What alternative to 'readmission' measures true functional reintegration into community life?**
  *Creating metrics beyond simple recurrence rates*
