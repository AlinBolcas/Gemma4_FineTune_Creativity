## Vanilla

That is such a fun decision! Choosing a name for a sausage dog (Dachshund) is a delightful process because they are such unique, charismatic, and often hilarious little creatures!

To give you the *perfect* suggestion, it helps to know a little about your dog. Is he/she:

* **Long and low** (a true wiener dog)?
* **Bold and goofy**?
* **Elegant and dignified**?
* **Sleepy and cuddly**?
* **A bit stubborn**?

In the meantime, I've broken down name ideas into several fun categories based on different vibes!

---

## 🌭🌭🌭 Food & Snack Inspired (The Classic Dachshund Vibe)
Because they are long, low, and sometimes look like delicious snacks!

* **Hot Dog / Wiener / Frank** (The obvious, but fun!)
* **Meatball**
* **Brat**
* **Tater** (For a cute, potato-like look)
* **Noodle** (Perfect for their long body)
* **Peanut** (If they are small and chunky)
* **Waffles**
* **Sausage / Sammy**

## 🐾🐈calico & Pattern Inspired (For the Unique Look)
If your dog has a distinct coat color or pattern.

* **Rolo** (Brown/reddish coats)
* **Pepper** (Black/dark coats)
* **Oreo** (Black and white)
* **Shadow** (For darker dogs)
* **Patches** (For multi-colored coats)
* **Copper** (For reddish tones)

## 👑👑Royal & Dignified (For the Little Prince/Princess)
Some Dachshunds have surprisingly big personalities!

* **Duchess / Duke**
* **Winston**
* **Regal**
* **Baron / Baroness**
* **Cleo** (Short for Cleopatra)
* **Prince / Princess**

## 🤪Funny & Quirky (For the Goofy Dog)
These names suit the little dogs with big personalities.

* **Wiggles**
* **Gizmo**
* **Sir Reginald Fluffington** (If you want a very long, funny name)
* **Weenie** (Classic!)
* **Gizmo**
* **Gherkin** (A fun, random vegetable name)
* **Turbo** (If they have surprising bursts of energy)

## 🌳Nature & Earthy (For the Adventurer)
If your dog loves sniffingishes or exploring!

* **River**
* **Moss**
* **Scout**
* **Oak**
* **Hickory**
* **Juniper** (Juni)
